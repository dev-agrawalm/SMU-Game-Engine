Proposal:
A galaga-style 2D scrolling game with an in-built spline editor for the enemy paths.
The game will feature a player in space, controlling a spaceship, that is being bombarded with enemies and bullets and the player has to survive/beat all the enemies.
The enemies will follow interesting and unique paths which will be defined by splines made in the spline editor.
The path editor will allow you to define splines for anything (its gonna be used for enemy paths primarily).
